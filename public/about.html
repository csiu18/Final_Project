About

Tufts Rides is a web app that allows Tufts students to see how far away they are from a certain stop as well as arrival times and schedule.


Map

The map shows you how far away you are from a stop.
You can choose which stop you want directions for, and it tells you the time it takes to get there.
So you can plan what time to leave in order to get to the station on time!


Arrival time

Below the map, there is a list of stops as well as the times until the next shuttle arrives.
The two times show the closest time to arrival, and the second closest time to arrival.
In this way, instead of looking at the schedule for the next shuttle, you can look right below the map!


Schedule

There's also the option to look at the whole schedule if you need to plan ahead.
